How to Play Hand & Foot

Set-up

Each player has a full deck of 54 cards (including 2 Jokers). All cards are shuffled together and dealt into piles of 15 cards that number double the number of players. Each player chooses 2 piles: one becomes the Hand while the other becomes the Foot. The foot is placed face-down to the side and the hand becomes your starting hand. The remaining cards form the draw pile, with the top card flipped to start the discard pile.

Turn Structure

To start the turn, draw from either the draw pile or the discard pile (taking the whole pile). Next, play all cards that you can. Note: To play your first set, the points must exceed 30 for the first round, 60 for the second, and so on. You must discard one card to end your turn. Play progresses clockwise.

Ending the Round

To end the round, a player must achieve all of the following:

  • 2 closed clean sets
  • 2 closed dirty sets
  • No cards remaining in the Foot
  • No cards remaining in the Hand
When these conditions are met and the player ends their turn, the round ends immediately. All scores are tallied and recorded before the next round begins.

Ending the Game

The game concludes once the desired number of rounds is finished (the standard is 3 rounds). After the final round is tallied, each player's total points from all rounds are added together. The player with the highest total score wins the game!
